My Console Site Plan

Zane Pickering

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this site is to give my audience an ability to choose what video game console is best for them. This will give opportunities to those new to video games to find specific information.

Audience

My audience are for for those that are new to video games or for those can't decide what console they want to use.

Dynamic elements

In my child page there will be a list of subjects for consoles such as: Cost, mobility, game accessibility, etc. On the right it will show the most mainstream consoles with 5 bars underneath each one. When the user clicks on one of the subjects, the bars underneath the consoles will fill up to show which ones are best for that subject in particular, as will show a description underneath as to why.
